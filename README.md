## 🎯 The Problem

Every year, thousands of SSB aspirants walk into the psychology round blind.

- **Coaching is expensive** — ₹15,000 to ₹50,000 for a psych course alone.
- **Feedback is rare** — you write stories and responses with zero idea if you're projecting the right personality.
- **OLQs are abstract** — knowing what "Effective Intelligence" means is very different from knowing if your TAT story actually demonstrates it.
- **One attempt wasted** — you only realize your psych was weak *after* you get screened out.

Most aspirants practice blindly. SSBGPT changes that.

---

## 💡 The Solution

**SSBGPT** is an AI-powered psychological analysis platform built specifically for SSB candidates. Upload your handwritten or typed test responses — TAT stories, WAT sentences, SRT answers, SD paragraphs, PIQ — and get a detailed, structured breakdown of your performance in seconds.

Think of it as having a **senior SSB psychologist** reviewing your work, mapping your OLQs, flagging weaknesses, and rewriting your responses for you — for free.

## ✨ Features

### 📖 TAT — Thematic Apperception Test
Upload handwritten stories, typed text, or a full PDF.
- Story structure check (Past → Present → Future)
- Hero clarity, theme positivity, stimulus match
- OLQ mapping per story
- Rewritten ideal version of your story
- Score out of 10 with justification

### 🔤 WAT — Word Association Test
Upload single or multiple WAT response sheets (image or PDF).
- Word count validation (max 6 words)
- Pronoun and positivity checks
- OLQ coverage map across all responses
- Corrected sentences for every weak response
- Overall WAT rating: Excellent / Good / Average / Below Average

### ⚡ SRT — Situation Reaction Test
Upload individual or bulk SRT responses.
- Situation category classification
- Realism and officer-quality evaluation
- OLQ signals per situation
- Improved response suggestions
- Overall SRT rating

### 🪞 SD — Self Description
Upload individual paragraphs or your full SD.
- Authenticity check — is it genuine or templated?
- OLQ evidence mapping across all 5 paragraphs
- Specific line replacements with better phrasing
- Rewritten paragraph per section
- Consistency analysis across your SD

### 📋 PIQ — Personal Information Questionnaire
Upload your full PIQ.
- Personality trait extraction
- Leadership indicator mapping
- Initial OLQ probability profile
- Key themes for interview anticipation

### 🧠 Full Psych Test Analysis
Upload your entire psych test as a PDF or set of images.
- Section-wise analysis (TAT + WAT + SRT + SD)
- Cross-test personality consistency check (Mansa-Vacha-Karma)
- Complete 15 OLQ assessment with scores
- SSB Readiness verdict: **Recommended / Needs Work / Not Ready**
- Priority improvement roadmap

---

## ⭐ Standout Features

### 🔄 Refresh Analysis
Not satisfied with the first output? Hit **Refresh Analysis**.

Every refresh generates a completely new analysis with:
- A different interpretation of your responses
- A freshly rewritten story/response
- New angle on OLQ signals

Use it to explore multiple perspectives on your performance and pick the framing that best represents your personality.

### 📄 Export as PDF
Every analysis can be exported as a clean, structured PDF — ready to save, print, or share.

Exported PDFs include:
- Full OLQ breakdown with scores
- Strengths and weaknesses
- All improvement suggestions
- Rewritten stories and responses

Use it to **track your progress** across attempts, bring it to a mentor, or just keep a record of where you stand before your actual SSB date.

## 🧑‍💻 Example: A Typical Session

**Scenario:** Arjun, 21, has his SSB in 3 weeks. He wrote 4 TAT stories last night.

1. He opens [ssbgpt.vercel.app](https://ssbgpt.vercel.app)
2. Goes to **TAT** → uploads his handwritten story sheet as a PDF
3. Gets a breakdown of all 4 stories: structure, theme, OLQ signals, score
4. Notices Courage and Initiative are consistently missing
5. Reads the AI-rewritten versions to understand the gap
6. Hits **Refresh** to get an alternate rewrite with a different story arc
7. Goes to **Full Analysis** → uploads his complete psych test PDF
8. Gets an overall OLQ rating and SSB Readiness verdict
9. **Exports the PDF** and saves it to revisit before the SSB

Total time: ~10 minutes. Total cost: ₹0.

## 🌍 Why This Project Matters

The SSB is one of the toughest selection processes in the world. The psychological round alone eliminates the majority of candidates — not because they lack leadership potential, but because they don't know how to *communicate* it on paper.

A ₹30,000 coaching course gives you that feedback. SSBGPT gives you the same feedback for free.

This isn't just a side project. For a 19-year-old from a Tier-2 city with no access to specialized coaching, this tool is a genuine equalizer. If it helps even a handful of capable candidates get recommended who otherwise wouldn't have — that's the whole point.

## ⚠️ Disclaimer

SSBGPT is an **AI-assisted practice tool**, not a substitute for the actual SSB process.

- Analyses are generated by AI and may not perfectly reflect the judgment of trained SSB psychologists.
- The SSB Readiness verdict is an **approximation**, not a guarantee.
- This platform is intended for **preparation and self-improvement** only.
- Always supplement AI feedback with real practice, peer review, and guidance from credible mentors.

Use it as a mirror — not as a verdict.
